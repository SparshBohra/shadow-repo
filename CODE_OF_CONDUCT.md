# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is a professional space. Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all discussions.
- Accept feedback on contributions without personal conflict.
- Focus criticism on code and ideas, not individuals.
- Assume good intent unless clearly demonstrated otherwise.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments.
- Harassment in any form, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who repeatedly violate these standards.
